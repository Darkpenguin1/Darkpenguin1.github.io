About Us

At Flores y Fresas, we believe every special moment deserves a touch of beauty and sweetness. Rooted in creativity and love, we specialize in handcrafted floral bouquets, elegantly themed chocolate-covered strawberries, and vibrant berry cups that make every occasion unforgettable. Whether you're celebrating love, friendship, birthdays, or just sending a thoughtful gesture, our arrangements are designed to delight the senses and warm the heart. Each product is thoughtfully made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a passion for presentation—because at Flores y Fresas, we don’t just create gifts, we craft moments.
