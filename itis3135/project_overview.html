Flores y Fresas

Project Overview

Application & Purpose: My application and its purpose is to promote an individuals flower business. The intended users of the application are potential customers that want to view the individuals work in photo gallerys, and to improve awareness of the individuals business. For a user story an ideal customer is a user who is around there 20s who can navigate a application freely. The overview of the content of the website is a about us page, stating how their business came into fruition. Another feature of the application is a photo gallery of the individuals flowers and offerings. The application will be able to filter the offerings of the users offerings for example filtering between roses and sunflowers within a boquet. Additional functionality of the application is that users could view upcoming sales of boquets or special offerings in the application.

Page Design

  • Home Page
    • Purpose: Main purpose of the page is for navigation and displaying a brief gallery of images.
    • Audience: Customers who first visit the page
    • Content: Hero img, navigation bar, and contact information
    • Will the page contain buttons or hyperlinks or drop downs? This page will include buttons and dropdowns
    • When the user clicks on navigation links they will be directed to the respective page. Interactive image that will carousel through 3 images
  • About Us
    • Purpose: Main purpose of the page is to show how the personal business started, and the specialities.
    • Audience: Customers who are interested in the story of the business
    • Content: Paragraph explaining how the business started and the personal connection to the business
    • The page will not include buttons and dropdowns
  • Gallery
    • Purpose: Main purpose of the page is to show the works of the business, boquets, chocolate strawberries etc.
    • Audience: Customers who want to view past works and as a admin to upload images of past works
    • Content: Images of past work and possibly a filter to filter via image types
    • Will the page contain buttons or hyperlinks or drop downs? This page will include buttons and dropdowns
    • When users filter via a drop down the page will hide images that do not include a certain id for example, filtering via boquets will hide all non boquet class items.
  • Services
    • Purpose: Main purpose of the page will be to display pricings and services of the business.
    • Audience: Customers who are interested in purchasing a service from the business
    • Content: Images, paragraphs, and drop down filters
    • When using drop downs to filter and hide classes similar to before. A table to display prices
  • Deals
    • Purpose: Main purpose of the page will be to display special offerings for the time period
    • Audience: Customers who are interested in purchasing special offerings
    • Content: Tables, Images, themed items
